The essential link between your smartphone and your watch

Customize the dials of your watch in a few clicks. Download new, exclusive TAG Heuer watch dials. Find all the sport sessions saved by your TAG Heuer Connected on the app, and synchronize with Apple Health for iOS users and Strava, as well. Your every performance analysed, with TAG Heuer elegance as a bonus.

 

TWO HISTORIES, ONE PASSION

These two icons of design, performance, and innovation have journeyed in parallel since 1963. The time has come to unite them on a single tack, with a sporty elegant TAG Heuer Carrera watch face, full of references to both brands. A historical name, born on the racetrack, which perfectly expresses our passion for the road with a dial's asphalt effect. Arabic numerals suggest the numbers on the dashboard of fine Porsche sportscars.

SET UP FOR SUCCESS

All your daily essentials at a glance: this digital watchface aims at providing everything you need in the most readable yet elegant way. With up to six complications available, makes the most of your daily life with the right information. Beautifully engineered, make your watch unique by pairing your strap and your watchface with a choice of eight different colors.

A SUN-FILLED DIAL, RADIANT AND ASSERTIVE

Select a classic mechanical look inspired by TAG Heuer iconic chronographs or go digital with three complications celebrating summer. It is offered in four variations of seasonal hues: deep blue, peppermint green, cherry red and dark summer night.

THE MOST ELEGANT DIAL, ROOTED IN TAG HEUER CARRERA HERITAGE

An elegant combination of our TAG Heuer Carrera heritage and our avant-garde vision. Across the dial, three different counters with mechanical or digital complications. Also available without complication and in classic colors, for a refined personalisation.

Rethinking time measurement

At the rim, a constantly moving neural network for a visionary representation of the seconds. At the centre, a hand or digital time display, for a dial unique the world over.

The Heuer 02 calibre dial revisited. The perfect mix between heritage and avant-garde.

We combined our mechanical heritage and our vision of the future in this tribute to the TAG Heuer Carrera Heuer 02. Three different counters with mechanical or digital complications, customizable with ease.

Our passion for measurement, our taste for avant-garde

The carbon molecule becomes a measuring tool. At the centre, a hand or digital time display. Across the dial, a fascinating molecular network indicates the progress towards your daily step goal.

A tribute to the most beautiful performance

It’s the precision of the chronometer that makes performance the stuff of legend. This dial is inspired by the legendary chronometers with liquid crystal display that marked our history and that of the greatest sports.
